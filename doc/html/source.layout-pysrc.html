 12  ''' 
 13  LayoutSpec 
 14   
 15  Specifications (parameters) for layout. 
 16  For menu layout. 
 17  For other composites? 
 18   
 19  Controls are transformed. 
 20  Scheme has a group of all controls not belonging to a graphical morph. 
 21  The transform of that group is the transform for top-level controls. 
 22  The transform only scales (according to user preference or degree of zoom?) 
 23   
 24  A menu is a group. 
 25  Thus the hierarchy of transforms for items is: 
 26    scheme control group transform (scales) 
 27    menu group transform (translates and rotates) 
 28    item transform (scales a unit glyph) 
 29    
 30  A LayoutSpec does not necessarily describe the actual layout. 
 31  For example, layout() may ignore vector in LayoutSpec and instead use a hardcoded vector. 
 32   
 33  Coordinates: currently in DCS. 
 34  A menu is drawn in DCS effectively since controls drawn in their own context. 
 35  FIXME should be in GCS of glyph?
